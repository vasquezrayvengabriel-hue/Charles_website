About De La Salle John Bosco College

Building lives through faith, service, excellence, and modern education.

OUR HISTORY

The Lasallian Legacy

De La Salle John Bosco College has remained committed to providing quality Catholic education inspired by the teachings of Saint John Baptist de La Salle.


Throughout the years, the institution has produced competent professionals, servant leaders, and responsible citizens who continue to make meaningful contributions to society.


Today, DLSJBC continues to embrace innovation while preserving the Lasallian values of Faith, Service, and Communion in Mission.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We provide quality, learner-centered, and values-driven education that nurtures academic excellence, leadership, innovation, and social responsibility in accordance with the Lasallian tradition.

We envision De La Salle John Bosco College as a leading educational institution producing globally competent, morally upright, and service-oriented graduates committed to nation-building.
